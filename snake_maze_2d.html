Snake Maze 2D: Labyrinth Challenge

Game Objective

Welcome to Snake Maze 2D! In this game, your primary mission is to control a snake and navigate through a series of challenging 2D labyrinths. You must guide the snake to eat all the food hidden in the maze to unlock the portal to the next level. Precision and planning are the keys to survival!

How to Play

  • Control the Snake: Use the Arrow keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to change the snake's moving direction.
  • Eat All Food: Explore the twisting paths of the maze and eat every piece of food scattered across the grid.
  • Unlock the Portal: Once all the food has been consumed, the secret portal to the next level will be unlocked and activated.
  • Reach the Exit: Guide your snake safely into the portal to complete the level and advance further.
  • Avoid Crash: Do not hit the maze walls or bite your own body, or it's game over immediately!
