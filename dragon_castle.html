Dragon Castle: Tower Defense

Game Objective

In "Dragon Castle: Tower Defense," your mission is to build defense towers to defeat all incoming alien monsters and protect the safety of the castle. The monsters are relentless, and only strategic tower placement will keep the castle safe from invasion.

How to Play

  • Build various types of defense towers along the path to stop the alien monsters.
  • Each tower has unique abilities, such as shooting, freezing, or poisoning enemies.
  • Upgrade your towers to make them stronger and more effective against tougher monsters.
  • Defeat all waves of monsters to protect the castle and win the game!
